🔴Every Round — Never Skip
▶
Does the environment react?
Fire spreading, ceiling crumbling, fog thickening, tide rising
What does the enemy WANT right now?
Not just "attack" — scared? Buying time? Protecting something?
Triggered abilities on turn start/end?
Auras, ongoing damage, condition timers, lair actions
Legendary actions available?
Use them — don't forget they exist
Narrate cinematically
"It lunges" not "it attacks." Make it feel dangerous.
🟠Start of Combat
▶
State the stakes out loud
What happens if they lose? What are they protecting?
Set the scene — one vivid detail
Sound, smell, the enemy's first move
Have the statblock open
HP, AC, recharge abilities — before initiative rolls
Identify the most dangerous player
Smart enemies target them or neutralize them early
Note environmental interactions
What can players use? What can backfire?
🟢During Combat
▶
Enemy reacts to damage
Rage, fear, desperation — behavior shifts as HP drops
Call out HP thresholds aloud
"It's wounded" at ~50%, "barely standing" at ~25%
Put someone in genuine danger
At least once — a player should feel the risk is REAL
Reward creative solutions
Clever ideas should work, or at least partially work
Enemy moves and repositions
They're fighting to survive too — use the terrain
Spread the pain — vary targets
Don't always hit the same player. Keep everyone nervous.
🔵End of Combat
▶
Describe the aftermath vividly
The silence after the last blow matters
Acknowledge what permanently changed
Something real happened. The world is different now.
Give players a beat to react
Don't rush to loot. Let the moment breathe.
Note threads opened by this fight
Clues found, enemies fled, witnesses present
🟣Stakes — Keep It Real
▶
What does losing COST them?
Death, capture, NPC loss, location destroyed, secret revealed
Is there a clock ticking?
Someone escaping, ritual completing, reinforcements arriving
Is an NPC in danger?
Players fight harder when protecting someone they care about
Can the enemy escape or call for help?
Retreat is a valid enemy action. Use it.
Someone should sweat today
Easy wins feel hollow. Earned wins feel legendary.
